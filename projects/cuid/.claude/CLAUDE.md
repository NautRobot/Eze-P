# Project Context

This is an AI agent workspace for aiding in the development and review of the **Component Unified Identifier (CUID)** project. It will be used to research topics related to CUID and aid in the development/review of the project.

# About Me

I am a developer on the CUID project. My audience consists of fellow developers and potential users of the CUID project, which may include developers of other AMD tools or system administrators who would make use of the CUID project to identify supported devices on their systems.
